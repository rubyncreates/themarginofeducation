Starting something new always feels disproportionate to the act of starting it. You write a sentence, and a sentence is just a sentence — but the moment before, there was nothing, and the moment after, there is.

This is the first entry. Future entries will probably be longer, or shorter, or about something specific. For now: a placeholder, a hello, a way of testing that the page renders.

## Why a blog

Because the alternative is silence, mostly. Because writing things down forces you to find out what you actually think. Because the open web is still the best place for ideas that don't fit anywhere else.
